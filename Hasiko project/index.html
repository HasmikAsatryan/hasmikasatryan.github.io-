About Me

-"Everything becomes possible with me."

I am a photographer and first assistant director (First AD) with experience of 4 years. I took 2 masterclasses from photographers from New York Gallery and New York times. I have also worked in 2 music videos as a First AD and participated in 10 workshops about filmmaking with specialists from various countries hosted by TUMO center. In 2019, I was a jury member of the Rolan international film festival for children's which took place in the Moscow cinema. I am also a producer with experience of 2 years. I've worked in short movies and have participated in masterclasses organized by Kinoket productions. I am a very active person, always on time and approaching to my work professionally.
